Welcome to Capiz

Capiz province located on Panay Island is a developing ecotourism destination that offers various

activities for every kind of traveler. Roxas City, the capital of Capiz, is recognized as the country's

Seafood Capital and one of the best food destinations in the Philippines.

Capiz tourist spots include historic and religious spots, off-the-beaten-path natural wonders like

islands and waterfalls, caves with gorgeous stalactites and stalagmites, and local handicrafts you can bring home.

Some of the must-visit attractions in Capiz include Baybay Beach, Olotayan Island, Mantalinga Island,

Palina Greenbelt Ecopark, Suhot Cave and Spring, and Malinamon Falls.
